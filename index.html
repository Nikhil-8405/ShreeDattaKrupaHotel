Our Story

A Legacy of Taste & Trust

Since 1986, Shree Datta Krupa Hotel has been a beloved part of the Vasai East community. Our traditional Maharashtrian kitchen serves fresh, hygienic, and homely food that keeps generations of customers coming back.

From a warm cup of cutting chai to a full thali lunch, we offer affordable meals prepared with authentic recipes and pure ingredients — just like home.
