Our Journey

The Shree Datta Krupa Story

In 1986, Shree Datta Krupa Hotel opened its doors in the heart of Vasai East with a simple mission — to serve fresh, homemade food at honest prices. What started as a small eatery near Rachana Industrial Estate quickly became a beloved dining spot for local workers, families, and businesses.

Over the decades, we've stayed true to our roots. Our kitchen follows traditional Maharashtrian recipes passed down through generations. Every dish — from our morning cutting chai to the full lunch thali — is prepared fresh daily with quality ingredients and genuine care.

Today, we serve not just individual diners but also provide bulk meal services for factories, offices, and corporate companies in the surrounding industrial area. Our reputation is built on consistency, hygiene, and the warmth of homely food.

Milestones

Our Journey Through the Years

1986

The Beginning

Started as a small tea and snack stall serving workers in the Vasai East industrial area. Fresh chai and vada pav became instant favorites.

1995

Expanded Menu

Introduced full meals, thali, and a variety of Maharashtrian dishes. Became a go-to lunch spot for daily workers and families.

2005

Corporate Catering Begins

Started bulk meal supply services for nearby factories and industrial offices, building long-term partnerships with local businesses.

2015

Renovated & Modernized

Upgraded the restaurant space with better seating, kitchen facilities, and hygiene practices while maintaining the homely atmosphere.

Today

35+ Years Strong

Serving 1000+ customers daily with the same passion, quality, and affordability that defined us from day one.
