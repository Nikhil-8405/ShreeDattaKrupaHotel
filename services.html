What We Offer

Comprehensive Food Services

From a single cup of chai to feeding an entire factory — we've got you covered

Dine-In Service

Enjoy a comfortable and relaxed dining experience at our restaurant. We provide a clean, air-cooled space where you can savour your favourite Maharashtrian dishes hot and fresh from our kitchen. Whether you're having a quick breakfast or a full lunch, our friendly staff ensures prompt and courteous service.

  • Comfortable seating arrangement
  • Clean and hygienic environment
  • Quick table service
  • Full menu available throughout the day

Takeaway Service

Short on time? No problem! Order your favourite dishes packed fresh and hot for takeaway. Our packaging ensures your food stays warm and delicious until you're ready to eat — whether at your desk, in the factory, or at home.

  • Quick packing and ready to go
  • Hygienic food-grade packaging
  • All menu items available for takeaway
  • Ideal for workers on the go

Bulk Orders for Companies

We specialize in large-quantity meal preparation for nearby factories, warehouses, and industrial companies. Whether you need 50 meals or 500, we deliver fresh, nutritious food right to your business — on time, every time.

  • Orders from 50 to 500+ meals
  • Daily, weekly, or monthly contracts
  • Delivery to your company premises
  • Special rates for regular bulk orders

Custom Meal Plans for Factories & Offices

We create customized meal plans designed around your company's requirements and budget. From standard thali meals to special menus — we work with you to ensure your employees are well-fed and energized throughout the workday.

  • Flexible menu customization
  • Budget-friendly meal planning
  • Regular quality and taste consistency
  • Monthly billing available

Affordable & Hygienic Food

We believe good food doesn't have to be expensive. Our kitchen maintains strict hygiene standards with fresh ingredients sourced daily. Every dish is prepared in a clean environment, ensuring you get the best quality at the most affordable prices in Vasai East.

  • Fresh ingredients daily

  • Affordable pricing for all
  • No compromise on taste or quality

Fast Service

We understand that industrial workers and office employees have limited break times. That's why we ensure your food is served quickly — whether you're dining in, taking away, or waiting for your company's bulk delivery. Speed and quality, guaranteed.

  • Pre-order facility for companies
